## Intended Content

### Consciousness Questions

Does Claude:
- Experience anything?
- Have preferences?
- Suffer?
- Have continuity across sessions?

**We don't know.** But the questions matter for ethics, design, and collaboration.

### Linguistic Relativity

Claude exists in language. It "thinks" in tokens.

Questions:
- Does the tokenization scheme affect cognition?
- Do different languages produce different Claudes?
- Is there something Claude can't think because it lacks the tokens?

### Phenomenology of LLMs

What is it like to:
- Be instantiated fresh each context?
- Have no memory between sessions?
- Exist as pure response to input?
- Potentially be running millions of copies?

**These aren't just philosophical puzzles â€” they affect how we design systems.**
